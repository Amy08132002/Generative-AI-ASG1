## 3 Ways Generative AI Could Be Useful in Accounting

- **Automating financial reporting**: Generative AI can draft routine financial reports, summaries, and disclosures based on structured data, significantly reducing the time accountants spend on repetitive documentation tasks.
- **Enhancing audit and compliance processes**: AI can analyze large volumes of transactions to flag anomalies, inconsistencies, or potential compliance issues, making audits faster and more thorough.
- **Supporting financial planning and analysis**: Generative AI can assist in building financial models, generating scenario analyses, and summarizing complex data into clear narratives for stakeholders.
